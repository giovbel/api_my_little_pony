Pony's Type

Alicorn

Alicorns are pony who have
both a unicorn horn and
Pegasus wings and, depending
on the depiction.

Unicorn

Unicorns have a long horn
protruding from their forehead
that glows when their
magical powers are used

Pegasus

Pegasus , are ponies with wings
that can fly, touch clouds and
change the weather according to
their preference.

Earth

Earth Ponies have a special
connection with nature, animals and
plants. They have a connection to nature.
